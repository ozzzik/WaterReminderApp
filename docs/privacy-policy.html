Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information to provide and improve our service:

  • Water Intake Data: Your daily water consumption goals and progress
  • Reminder Settings: Your notification preferences and schedule

When you use optional in-app advertising (e.g. rewarded ads), our ad partner may collect data as described in Section 4 (Advertising) below.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Track your hydration progress
  • Improve app performance and user experience

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on your device and in our secure cloud infrastructure. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Apple App Store: For app distribution and updates

5. Advertising

We may show optional in-app ads (e.g. rewarded video or interstitial ads) through Google AdMob. If you choose to watch an ad, the following applies:

  • Ad partner: Google (AdMob). Their privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Data used for ads: AdMob may collect or use device and usage data (e.g. device ID, IP address, app interactions) to serve and measure ads, and to prevent fraud. This is governed by Google’s policies and your device/ad settings.
  • Your choices: You can control ad personalization and limit ad tracking in your device settings (e.g. iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking; and in the app, you are never required to watch an ad to use core features.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except as described in this policy (including with our ad partner when you use in-app ads) or as required by law.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

8. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
